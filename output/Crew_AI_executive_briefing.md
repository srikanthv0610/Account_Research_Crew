# Executive Briefing: CrewAI

## Company Snapshot
- **Name:** CrewAI  
- **Industry:** Enterprise software, AI infrastructure, multi-agent orchestration.  
- **Founded:** 2024  
- **Headquarters:** Middletown, Delaware (remote-first).  
- **Stage:** Series A (~$18M raised); ~50–100 employees.  
- **Core Offering:** Platform to design, orchestrate, and run multi-agent AI systems for automating complex workflows across enterprises.  

---

## Recent Developments
1. **Thought Leadership:**  
   - Published reference architecture for production AI agents (Mar 2026).  
   - Released “AI Agent Survey 2026” showing trust/integration as top barriers for adoption.  
2. **Partnership Expansion:**  
   - Collaborations with Konecta (CX/BPO), KT (telco), and Cloudera (data platforms).  
3. **Traction:**  
   - Claimed usage by ~60% of U.S. Fortune 500 (likely pilots/POCs).  

---

## Key Priorities
1. **Scaling Enterprise Deployments:** Converting Fortune 500 pilots into broader rollouts.  
2. **Trust and Governance:** Addressing enterprise concerns around security, observability, and compliance.  
3. **Vertical Expansion:** Building solutions for CX/BPO, telco, and data-heavy industries via partners.  
4. **Differentiation:** Reinforcing position as a neutral orchestration layer across models and systems.  

---

- **Enterprise Architecture Fit:** Highlight how your offering complements their platform’s governance, observability, or integration capabilities.

- **Scaling GTM:** Discuss ways to help CrewAI expand implementation capacity or reach new verticals.  
- **Category Leadership:** Explore co-marketing opportunities like joint research, webinars, or case studies.
